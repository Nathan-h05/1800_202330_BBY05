Saftey Quote of the day:

How does Local Emergency assist you?


  • Create posts on accidents or hazardous events you witness and your post will display on the map to warn others.
  • See other user's posts so as you travel the city you can check your area to see if anyone has reported hazards within that area.
  • Help keep yourself and others more aware of potiential danger in your city

  • Go to the Add icon on your Navbar
  • Take a photo of the hazard --> then fill out Title, Serverity and Description
  • Press on the Submit button and your post will be added to the map, and photo library.

  • Select the 'Map' button on your Navbar
  • The 'Blue Circle' is your current location, from which point you can zoom out and view your posts and other peoples posts, represented by 'Red Event Pins'.
  • After clicking on an Event Pin you can select 'Read More' to be taken to a page displaying the post.
